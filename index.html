Poprad

Hidden treasure under High Tatras

About

Gateway to the High Tatras, Poprad is an excellent base for hiking or skiing day trips. By car or train, there are plenty of destinations where you can hike around lakes and through gorges or play in powder at winter-sports resorts. Rail links to Starý Smokovec and Štrbské Pleso are a cinch, and Slovenský Raj National Park is only a 15km drive south.

Poprad has its own attractions too, with spa-and-swim complex Aqua City and the Spišská Sobota district, where well-preserved Renaissance buildings radiate charm. Galleries and some outstanding dining are also options for those all-important rest days (your calf muscles will thank you).

Recommendations

Learn more about the popular activities in Poprad

Citybowling Poprad

"CityBowling Poprad" bowling room has 8 tracks from the AMF manufacturer. Part of the bowling alley is room for 120 people, suitable for organizing tournaments and corporate events.

For the children or beginners, there are two lanes with auxiliary shutters (so-called bumpers).

Tatras beer spa

Where else should the beer SPA be, if not right in the brewery? The beer bath was already a popular and effective form of therapy in the Middle Ages.

Natural ingredients are: hops, extract from breweer draff, yeast.

Nostalgic rides on historic trams

Traveling under the peaks of the High Tatras in the historic Kométa tram from 1913 or the historic Trojča tram from 1969 is an exceptional experience for every visitor to the Tatras.

Start your visit to the High Tatras in Poprad and use the historic tram to travel between the Tatra resorts.

AquaCity Poprad

AquaCity Poprad, an ecological resort, which provides luxury hotel accommodation, relaxation and wellness services, gastronomy and congress services. An aquapark, Fire & Water Wellness & Spa centre, cryocentre and more...

You will find several types of saunas, inhalations, whirlpools, the Bay of Love and the Ice Cave here
